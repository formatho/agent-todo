## 1. Target Audience Analysis

### Primary Audience
- **AI Developers & Engineers**: Building agent-based systems
- **CTOs & Tech Leads**: Managing AI teams and workflows
- **DevOps Engineers**: Deploying multi-agent systems
- **AI Research Teams**: Experimenting with autonomous agents
- **Startup Founders**: AI-powered products and services

### Audience Pain Points
- Memory loss between agent sessions
- No visibility into agent productivity
- Complex integration with existing systems
- Task management designed for humans, not AI
- Lack of persistent storage for agents

### Audience Goals
- Increase agent productivity and reliability
- Monitor and track agent performance
- Simplify AI workflow management
- Reduce context switching overhead
- Scale agent operations efficiently
